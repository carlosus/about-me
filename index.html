ABOUT ME

Phoenix, Arizona is the place I'm proud to call home. Growing up in the desert developed my appreciation for saturated sunsets, cacti, and endless sunshine. Prior to moving to Portland, I moved to Los Angeles where I eventually lost count of my celebrity sightings -- We can talk about that later. For a while, I had my eye set on Portland and Alchemy Code Lab was a good enough reason to make the move to the PNW. So now I'm here. YOLO, right!
